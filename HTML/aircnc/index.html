About this Dog
The Golden Retriever is a large-sized breed of dog bred as

gun dogs to retrieve shot waterfowl such as ducks and

upland game birds during hunting and shooting parties

and were named 'retriever' because of their ability to

retrieve shot game undamaged. Golden Retrievers have an

instinctive lover of water and are easy to train to basic or

advanced obedience standards.
